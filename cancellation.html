Cancellation Policy

Guidelines for Cancelling Services

This policy explains how customers can cancel services and any applicable conditions or fees.

1. How to Cancel a Service

To request a cancellation, please send an email to contact@jkindrix.dev with your order details.

Cancellations must be requested before the start of service execution.

2. Refunds on Cancellations

If a service has not yet commenced, a full refund will be issued. Once work has started, refunds may be subject to a processing fee.

3. Automatic Renewals

If a service includes automatic renewals, customers may opt out at any time by submitting a cancellation request before the renewal date.

4. Early Termination Fees

For long-term projects or subscription-based services, an early termination fee may apply if cancellation occurs after a predefined commitment period.
